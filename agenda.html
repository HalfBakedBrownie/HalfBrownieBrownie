BACKGROUND PAPER ON 2022 1A3 WORLD-WIDE SENIOR ENLISTED LEADER (SEL) CONFERENCE

Purpose

To advocate for a 1A3 World-Wide SEL Conference and lay out expectations for talking points at the 2022 conference.

Background

The evolving Air Force environment has set the requirement for compound solutions to achieve lasting and durable successes for the 1A3 AFSC. Over the last 10 years, we have seen several AFSC mergers into the 1A3 community that have created complexity across different mission sets, coupled with geographical and communication barriers forcing leaders to formulate decisions within a narrowing scope. This diversity in mission, geography and a lack of exposure to the various missions within our community have reduced Senior Leaders’ collective ability to see and approach what are so often shared enterprise-wide challenges. The limited vantage points within MAJCOMs and MDS’s reduce our ability to address issues from a strategic level, without a collective voice, resulting in 1A3 talent management, CFETP evolution, and functional AFSC road mapping challenges. If we fail to unify we lose the ability to collectively steer decisions, formulate overarching AFSC strategies, and synchronize efforts. When combined, these generate increased risks that compound and lead to unintended or uncontrolled career field-wide

Discussion

To overcome such challenges and to take advantage of the 1A3 AFSC’s unique diversity as an enterprise, we recommend the normalization of a 1A3 Senior Leadership Conference to widen our network and discuss community wide initiatives and solutions. We must ask what type of experiences, education and training the Air Force and nation require to secure a strategic, operational and intellectual overmatch through 2040 and against our near-peer competitors. This event offers a forum to usher in a network to reorient the 1A3 community for the next fight.

Challenges

  • Uncertain Future Environment/Uncertain AFSC Future/Direction
    • Fiscal Constraints
    • Strategic Competition
    • Growth/Divesture of platforms
  • Talent Management
  • Career-Field Wide Unity

Oppertunities/achieving Integration

  • Decision-Making (Planning, Force Management, Force Development/Design)
    • Shared understanding of problem sets
    • Provide perspective for coordinating resources
    • Developing Career Enlisted Aviators
    • Align communities at appropriate echelons
    • Identify advanced technology commonalities and unify training solutions

Recommend

SELs from the Sq, Gp, Wg, and all staff levels assemble at JBSA-Lackland, TX for the 1 st annual 1A3 World-Wide SEL Conference.
